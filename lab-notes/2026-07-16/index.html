The wiggle budget is the causal structure: reach goes 1.26 → √2 → 2, and there is no horizon

Closed-form analysis (no campaign) · analysis module + tests bc2d4b9 · shipped as the frame map's default front in wrapped viewer v27 1ab48c2

Chris questioned the conformal fit speed the frame map used (v26, 07-15): instead of a pulse whose comoving radius grows from 0 like c·Δη, he argued the reach should be roughly constant (≈1) up to π/2 and grow to 2 only afterwards, “because you can connect comoving x=1 to x=0 at any point before π/2 with a high frequency function” — guessing the values ~1.2128 → ~1.3333 → 2. He is right, and the numbers are closed forms. A unit-budget term (|a·b| = 1, a = 1/b) sits at X(z) = [sin(bz+f) − sin f]/(b·sin z), which is linear in (sin f, cos f) — so the maximum comoving displacement between any two times is exact per frequency, and budget linearity means the best single term beats any multi-term split.

  • Chris's numbers decoded. The phase-free (f = 0) ensemble has a Bang plateau of exactly 1 + |min sinc| = 1.21723 at bz = 4.4934 (the root of tan u = u) — his ~1.2128. At π/2 the winner is b = 3 giving exactly 4/3 — his ~1.3333. And at the Crunch every even frequency reaches exactly 2. His “ignore the part that dips below x=0” is exact too: stopping the path at its zero crossing (bz = π) gives reach exactly 1; the turnaround overshoot is the extra 0.217.
  • The current ensemble is slightly hotter. With free phases on even frequencies (always on since 07-09) the plateau lifts to 1.25959 (bz = 4.086) and the π/2 value to √2 (b = 2, f = π/4). Late growth is the b = 2 envelope 2 sin(z/2), which overtakes the plateau at z ≈ 0.434π — the transition starts just before the turnaround.
  • No horizon at any epoch. The plateau exceeds the antipode distance χ = 1, so antipodal comoving points are connectable arbitrarily close to the Bang (needs b ≈ 4.1/z, well inside the sampled band b ≤ T). What changes with epoch is the width of the doubled-image band: ~0.26 early, growing to a fully doubled sky at the Crunch.
  • No conformal speed reproduces this (right panel): c·Δη diverges toward the Bang (over-reaching — the ghost-wrap images of v22–v26) and under-reaches at intermediate times for late observers. The model's causal structure is set by the wiggle budget, not a null cone.
  • Shipped: viewer v27's frame map now defaults to a “wiggle budget” front whose radius at rewound time ζ is R(ζ, zobs), natively a Chebyshev square (the reach is per-axis). The JS and Python implementations agree at t = 0.25/0.50/0.75/0.98π (χmax 1.21/1.37/1.82/2.00 with the 0.02π floor and b ≤ 180). The conformal fit-speed and speed-scalar modes remain as options.
